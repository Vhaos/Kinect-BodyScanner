Kinect Bodyscanner

User Manual
  • Begin by pressing "Start"
  • The app will attempt to open a connection to the Kinect Sensor, displaying feedback to the user regarding pose and scan distance. (Note: if the app fails to receive data frames, you will be asked to ensure you that the sensor is connected and working properly)
  • After moving closer/further away as needed, and adopting the correct pose, the app will provide a three second countdown before scanning.
  • The app then stores 3D information as a point-cloud, which it passes to the server for processing.
  • You will be presented with a custom QR code for you to scan with your android device.
  • The android app will occasionally check-in with the server to see if your measurements are ready. Once they are, the app will let you know!
  • Measurements are only stored on your device- letting you check them any time.
